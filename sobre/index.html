About

Love Letters was born from the need to write. To say simple things. To keep words close.

These are love letters meant to exist beyond paper: to be worn, to be touched, to live in everyday life.

Every piece begins with a text. A short poem. A sentence. An idea that asks for a body.

Love Letters does not try to explain everything. It prefers to leave space. For the reader. For the giver. For the receiver.
